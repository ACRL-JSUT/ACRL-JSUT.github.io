Associate Professors

  • Youwu Du

 

Associate Professor of the School of Electrical and Information Engineering,
Jiangsu University of Technology.
IEEE Senior Member.
He received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in engineering from Central South University, Changsha, China, in 2007 and 2010, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in engineering from China University of Geosciences, Wuhan, China, in 2020.
In 2012, he joined the School of Physics and Electronic Information, Anhui Normal University, Wuhu, China. He was a Visiting Scholar with the Graduate School of Industrial Technology, Advanced Institute of Industrial Technology, Tokyo, Japan, from 2018 to 2019. He is currently a Visiting Scholar with the School of Engineering, Tokyo University of Technology, Tokyo, from 2024 to 2025. In 2021, he moved to Jiangsu University of Technology, Changzhou, China, where he is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Electrical and Information Engineering. His research interests include disturbance estimation and rejection, application of control theory, robot control, and robust control.

  • Bo Li

 

Associate Professor of the School of Electrical and Information Engineering,
Jiangsu University of Technology.
He received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering and automation from Northwestern Polytechnical University, China in 2007 and the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from Nanjing University of Science & Technology, China in 2015. Now he works in School of Electrical & Information Engineering, Jiangsu University of Technology. His research work is in the areas of robust control, stochastic systems control and so on. He has published papers as the first author in journals such as AMC, TIMC, IFAC. He got funding from the National Natural Science Foundation of China in 2019.
